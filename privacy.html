Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026
Plusly (https://plusly.im)

Introduction

Plusly is a Matrix chat client. We do not collect or store any personal data on our own servers. All messages are stored on your chosen Matrix homeserver.

Permissions Used

  • CAMERA: Used only when you take a photo to send in a chat, or scan a QR code for login verification
  • MICROPHONE: Used for voice/video calls (with your consent during calls)
  • STORAGE: Used to save attachments you choose to download
  • LOCATION: Optional - used only if you choose to share your location

Data Storage

Your chat history, contacts, and media are stored on the Matrix homeserver you connect to. Plusly does not have its own servers.

Third Parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • Matrix.org homeserver (optional, just for testing)
  • Google Firebase (for push notifications, optional)
  • Unified Push (for unified push notifications)

Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time by removing your account from the app. All data on homeservers is controlled by the server administrator.

Contact
