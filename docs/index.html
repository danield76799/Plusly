Why Plusly?

Everything you need for modern communication, wrapped in a beautiful interface.

End-to-End Encrypted

Your messages are secured with full E2E encryption by default. Private by design.

Multiple Accounts

Switch seamlessly between personal, work, and community Matrix accounts without logging in and out.

Scheduled Sending

Plan your messages ahead of time. Perfect for birthday wishes, reminders, or sending messages at the perfect moment.

Bridge Translation

Chat across Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, and more with real-time automatic translation between bridges.

Unified Push

All your notifications in one place via Sunup or Ntfy. Stay notified without the battery drain of proprietary push services.

Compact Chat List

More conversations visible at once with our optimized chat list design. Find conversations faster.
